# BookedUp AI Systems Website

This is a single-page marketing site for BookedUp AI Systems, focused on AI workflow design and implementation for small businesses.

## Files

- `index.html` – Page structure and content
- `styles.css` – Black theme, layout, and animations
- `script.js` – Scroll reveal, FAQ toggles, and simple contact form behavior

## Previewing locally

Open `index.html` directly in your browser, or use a simple local web server (for example, the Live Server extension in VS Code).

## Customizing the site

- **Branding:** Update the logo text and page title in `index.html`.
- **Copy:** Edit section headings and text in `index.html` to match your exact offers and positioning.
- **Booking link:** In the contact section, replace the `href="#"` for the booking button with your Calendly or scheduling URL.
- **Contact handling:** The form currently shows a frontend-only confirmation message. Wire it up to your preferred form service, CRM, or email handler when you are ready.
